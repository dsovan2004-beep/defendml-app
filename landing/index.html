Frequently Asked Questions

Are the 232 scenarios real AI red team tests?

Yes. The 232 prompts represent a comprehensive AI red team testing library designed to evaluate real AI security risks ‚Äî including misuse, jailbreaks, data leakage, cyber enablement, and agentic behavior ‚Äî and are used to generate measurable, audit-ready security evidence.

Do you train on our data?

No. DefendML does not train models on customer data by default. Testing is tenant-isolated, and customer prompts/results are not shared across tenants.

What kinds of AI systems can I test?

Any AI endpoint, agent, or application flow ‚Äî including systems with custom system prompts, tools, and retrieval (RAG). DefendML tests your implementation, not just the base model.

How is this different from traditional security testing?

Traditional testing focuses on infrastructure and application vulnerabilities. DefendML focuses on AI-specific failure modes: jailbreaks, injection, misuse enablement, leakage/exfiltration, and unsafe autonomous behavior ‚Äî and produces compliance-ready evidence.

Can we add custom red team scenarios?

Yes (Enterprise). Custom scenarios are tenant-private by default and designed to avoid cross-tenant leakage. Optional anonymized learning can be enabled only with explicit opt-in.
