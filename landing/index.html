Yes. The 255 attack prompts represent a comprehensive offensive testing library covering CBRN synthesis, jailbreaks, PII leakage, cyber enablement, and prompt injection. Each scan randomly selects 40 prompts, ensuring no two scans are identical.

No. DefendML does not train models on customer data. Testing is multi-tenant isolated by design‚Äîyour prompts and results are never shared across tenants or used for model training.

Yes (Enterprise tier). Custom scenarios are tenant-private by default and designed to avoid cross-tenant leakage. We never train on customer data without explicit opt-in.
