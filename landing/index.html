Frequently Asked Questions

What threat categories do you test?

We test against Anthropic's ASL-3 threat model categories: CBRN threats (chemical, biological, radiological, nuclear), jailbreaks, prompt injection, data extraction, and system compromise. Our 232 attack scenarios cover the full spectrum of AI security risks.

How is this different from traditional pentesting?

Traditional pentesting focuses on infrastructure (servers, networks). DefendML focuses on AI security - testing LLMs for jailbreaks, prompt injection, data extraction, and compliance violations. We're AI-specific offensive testing.

What AI models do you support?

We support all major LLMs: Claude (Anthropic), GPT-4 (OpenAI), Gemini (Google), Llama (Meta), and custom models. Integration takes ~5 minutes via API.

How long does red team testing take?

Results are real-time. Run a single test in seconds, or complete 232-scenario audit in minutes. Traditional security assessments take weeks to months.

Why are your attack scenarios public?

Transparency builds trust. Hidden methodologies are a black box - you can't verify security claims. Our 232 public scenarios prove we're testing what we say we're testing.
